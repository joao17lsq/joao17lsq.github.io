Ibirapuera Park

Ibirapuera Park is an urban park in São Paulo. It comprises 158 hectares
(approx. 390 acres) between
Av. República do Líbano, Av. Pedro Alvares Cabral, and
Av. IV Centenário, and is the most visited park in
South America, with 14.4 million visits in 2017.
